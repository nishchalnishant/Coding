# Google SDE-2 (L4) Roadmap (4–6 Weeks)

This roadmap is optimized for **Google SDE-2**: strong coding rounds, enough system design to be safe, and focused behavioral prep.

Targets (typical):
- **Coding**: 60–90 high-quality problems, mostly Medium (some Easy + some Hard)
- **Mocks**: 6–10 sessions (45–60 min each)
- **System design**: 6–10 full run-throughs (30–45 min each)
- **Behavioral**: 6–8 STAR stories, practiced out loud

---

## Week 0 (setup — 1 day)

- Pick language (Python/Java) and lock templates (DFS/BFS, heap, binary search, DP).
- Create a simple **mistake log**: see `PRACTICE_TRACKER.md`.
- Skim `CODING_ROUNDS.md` and practice “talking while coding”.

---

## Week 1 (arrays/strings/hashing + windows)

Goal: become fast at the most frequent Google patterns.

- Do 15–20 problems from:
  - Arrays + prefix sums
  - Two pointers
  - Sliding window
  - Hash map / frequency
- 1 mock at end of week (2 problems).

Revision: `../foundations/GOOGLE_INTERVIEW_REVISION.md` sections B1–B2 + `../patterns/two-pointers-sliding-window.md`.

---

## Week 2 (trees + binary search + stack/queue)

- Trees: traversal, LCA, BST basics, tree DP “return multiple values”
- Binary search: lower/upper bound, rotated array, BS on answer
- Stack/queue: monotonic stack, deque
- Do 15–20 problems.
- 1 mock.

---

## Week 3 (graphs + BFS patterns)

- Graph basics: BFS/DFS, components, topo
- Grid BFS/DFS and multi-source BFS
- Shortest path: BFS vs Dijkstra (basic)
- Do 12–18 problems.
- 2 mocks (one graph-heavy).

---

## Week 4 (DP + backtracking + mixed)

- DP core: 1D/2D, LIS/LCS-style, knapsack/coin change
- Backtracking: permutations/combinations, pruning, recursion state
- Do 12–18 problems.
- 2 mocks (mixed).

---

## Week 5 (system design + polish) — optional but recommended

- Do 6–10 system design run-throughs from `SYSTEM_DESIGN_L4.md`.
- Do 6–10 coding problems you previously failed (from your mistake log).
- 2 mocks.

---

## Week 6 (behavioral + final readiness) — optional

- Finalize STAR stories using `BEHAVIORAL_GOOGLINESS.md`.
- Do 2 mixed mocks under strict timing.
- Only review mistakes; don’t learn new topics.
